I'm a guitarist, singer and songwriter. I first picked up a guitar at the age of 11, and I've been in love with it ever since. I try and play as many styles as I can, dabbling in jazz, funk, country, folk... but my main love and passion is (and probably always will be) rock and pop music. I started singing out of necessity (my band needed a singer) when I was around 16, and similarly, I started writing songs because we needed stuff to play! That band was called Stars & Flights, and we played together for about 5 years before calling it a day. I'm currently writing and demoing material for a new band. I play as a session guitarist, working in pit orchestras and tribute bands. I also play bass guitar and (attempt to) play the piano. I'm a qualified secondary school music teacher as well as a private guitar teacher. Head over to my contact page if you want to hire me or discuss guitar lessons!
