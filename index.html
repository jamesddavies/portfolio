music

James Davies Guitar Tuition

I first picked up a guitar at the age of 11 and I've been in love with it ever since. I try and play as many styles as I can, dabbling in jazz, funk, country, folk... but my main love and passion is (and probably always will be) rock and pop music. I started singing out of necessity (our band needed a singer) when I was around 16, and similarly, I started writing songs because we needed stuff to play! That band was called Stars & Flights, and we played together for about 5 years before calling it a day. I'm currently writing and demoing material for a new band. I also play as a session guitarist, working in pit orchestras and tribute bands.
