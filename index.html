I'm a guitarist, singer and songwriter. I first picked up a guitar at the age of 11, and I've been in love with it ever since. I try and play as many styles as I can, dabbling in jazz, funk, country, folk... but my main love and passion is (and probably always will be) rock and pop music. I started singing out of necessity (my band needed a singer) when I was around 16, and similarly, I started writing songs because we needed stuff to play! That band was called Stars & Flights, and we played together for about 5 years before calling it a day. I'm currently writing and demoing material for a new band. I play as a session guitarist, working in pit orchestras and tribute bands. I also play bass guitar and (attempt to) play the piano. I'm also a private guitar teacher. Head over to my contact page if you want to hire me or discuss guitar lessons!

design

I'm learning through a combination of online tutorials (courtesy of FreeCodeCamp and Codecademy, among others) and building my own projects, such as this website. I'm always open to constructive criticism so if you have any, please head over to my Contact page and get in touch! I'm loving the learning process so far (especially the problem solving nature of coding and web development) and I'm hoping to take the skills I learn into a career in web development and design.

about

I'm James, I'm 23, and I'm from Neath in South Wales. When I'm not playing music, building websites or writing, you can usually find me watching terrible TV, hanging out with friends or hiking up big hills. I'm a graduate of Bristol Institute of Modern Music and a qualified secondary school music teacher. My current aim is simply to become better at developing high quality, fully responsive websites and web apps. I've only just started, but I'm getting there!
